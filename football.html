Football - The Beautiful Game

Experience the passion, skill, and excitement of the world's most popular sport.

About Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal.

With a rich history dating back over 2,000 years, football has evolved into the world's most popular sport, played and watched by millions across the globe.

Did You Know?

  • The first World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay.
  • A regulation football field is between 100-130 yards long and 50-100 yards wide.
  • The fastest goal in football history was scored in just 2.1 seconds!

Football Competitions

FIFA World Cup

The most prestigious international football tournament, held every four years.

UEFA Champions League

An annual club competition for the top European teams.

English Premier League

One of the most-watched football leagues, featuring top English clubs.

Copa America

The main football competition among national teams in South America.

Basic Football Rules

Key Rules of Football

  • Two teams of 11 players each compete to score goals.
  • The game is played over two 45-minute halves.
  • Players cannot use their hands or arms to control the ball (except for goalkeepers within their penalty area).
  • Offside rule: A player is in an offside position if they are nearer to the opponents' goal line than the ball and the second-last opponent.
  • Fouls result in free kicks or penalty kicks, depending on where they occur.
  • Yellow cards are given as warnings, and red cards result in player dismissal.
  • The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.
